Biography of Tzeke000

❮ ❯

Introduction

Ezekiel, known professionally as Tzeke000, is a dynamic and innovative music artist who has captivated audiences with his unique blend of genres and powerful storytelling. Born and raised in the Carlotte, North Carolina area, Tzeke000's musical journey began at a young age, inspired by a diverse array of influences ranging from classic rock, contemporary pop, to classical music.

Early Life

Tzeke000 was born and raised in the Charlotte area. From a young age, they were influenced by classical music and instruments like the piano and tuba.

Musical Journey

Tzeke000 began their musical career in 2016 and released their first song Çrønexa on sound cloud in 2019. Later the first album Dub Brain was put on all music streaming sites in 2020.

Influences

Tzeke000 draws inspiration from artists such as Marshmellow, Flume, San Holo, Au5, and deadmou5, and genres including Future Bass, EDM, and pop.

Personal Touch

Outside of music, Tzeke000 enjoys playing games, playing airsoft, and working on his car. They believe that when game is over the king and the pawn go back into the same box.
